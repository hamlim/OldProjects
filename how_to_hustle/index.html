What is Hustle?

Or: What it means to Hustle

Hustle is the attitude that one takes when approaching a certain long term task or goal. Or at least that is how I see it, manny see hustle as a state of maximum effort applied to make ones self better at doing something. Here is a great breakdown of what Hustle means for entrepreneurs and founders: Hustle Explained.

Hustle = Hard Work + Determination + More Hard Work

Why I Started This:

Recently I have been listening to the Slow Hustle Podcast by Peter Awad and I think it is awesome that someone is documenting what founders will do to hustle and to also slow down when needed. Listening to this podcast was the inspiration that got me thinking about this side project, I highly recommend anyone interested to give it a listen! I figured it would be great if there was a way for founders and entrepreneurs to learn new methods to help them hustle more, and hustle more efficiently day to day.
